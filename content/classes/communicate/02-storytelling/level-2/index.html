Excercise

You should watch/listen to you assigned story a few times and take notes on the following:

  • What is key climate topic is being addressed?
  • What type of story is this story? Is the story wrapped up by the end of the piece?
  • Who are the characters who are taking action? What are they doing?
  • What elements of effective communication that we explored in the previous module are on display here? What is less effective about the mode of delivery?
  • How could you tell this story differently?

In class, you will meet with your peers assigned the same story and gather your ideas together to present to the group.
