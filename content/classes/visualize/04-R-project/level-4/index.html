For this level, I’m going to ask you to reflect on your project work and the visualize module more generally.

In no more than 2 pages (12pt, double-spaced), you should reflect on:

  • What you thought of your final visualization - What were you happy with? What were you disappointed with?

  • What changes you would make to what you have given more time/understanding.

  • What further development you could imagine for this kind of data display (doesn’t matter if you don’t know if this would be possible).

  • The process of creating the visualization: What key takeaways will you have from going through this project work (R, data design, personal workflow, etc.)

  • The use of a coded language in generating visualizations.
